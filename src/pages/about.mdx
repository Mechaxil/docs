# About Mechaxil

**Mechaxil** is an open-source electronics brand from **Hungary**, focused on **affordable kits**, **transparent documentation**, and **hands-on learning**.

## What we do
- Microcontroller boards (Raspberry Pi Pico, ESP32), sensors, power modules  
- Robotics & smart-agriculture starter kits  
- Step-by-step documentation (EN/HU) and example projects

## Our values
- **Open**: designs, code, and learning materials are shared whenever possible  
- **Accessible**: student- and hobbyist-friendly pricing  
- **Practical**: short, testable examples first; deep dives second
